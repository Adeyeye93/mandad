Our Mission

Helping People Stay Independent at Home

Mandad Medical Supply was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves to live comfortably and independently in their own home, regardless of age or physical limitation.

We are not a large national chain. We are your neighbours — people who understand the community, who answer the phone when you call, and who genuinely care about finding the right solution for your situation.

People First
Every decision we make is guided by what is genuinely best for our customers and their families.
Honest Advice
We recommend what we would choose for our own family — not the most expensive option on the shelf.
Community Rooted
We live and work in Woodbridge. This is not just a business location — it is our home too.
