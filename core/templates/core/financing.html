Questions & Answers

Financing FAQs

What credit score do I need to qualify?
The 0% interest plans typically require a credit score of 640 or above. Our extended plans are accessible from around 580. We work with multiple lenders so we can often find an option even for customers with challenged credit history.
Will applying affect my credit score?
The initial pre-qualification is a soft credit inquiry and will not affect your score. Only when you accept and finalise a plan will a hard inquiry be made.
Can I pay off the loan early?
Yes, all of our financing plans allow early payoff with no prepayment penalty.
Does Medicare cover any of the cost?
Medicare Part B covers 80% of approved durable medical equipment costs with a physician's prescription. We handle Medicare billing directly.
Can I combine financing with a rental credit?
Yes. If you have been renting equipment from us, we can apply a portion of your rental payments as a down payment toward a financed purchase.
