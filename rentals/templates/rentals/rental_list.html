Common Questions

Rental FAQs

What is the minimum rental period?
Our minimum rental period is one week. Most customers rent monthly, which offers the best value.
How does delivery and pickup work?
Delivery and pickup are free throughout Northern Virginia. Our team delivers, sets up the equipment in your home, and walks you through how to use it.
What if the equipment needs repair during my rental?
All repairs and maintenance are fully covered during your rental at no extra cost.
Can I apply my rental payments toward a purchase?
Yes. If you decide to purchase the item you are renting, we can apply a portion of your rental payments toward the purchase price.
How do I end my rental?
You can end your rental at any time with 30 days notice. We schedule a convenient pickup window. No early termination fees.

Not Sure What You Need?

Our team is happy to help you choose the right equipment for your situation.
